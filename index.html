55 to 65 days are needed from germination to harvest of the root. The usually deep purpose roots of beetroot are eaten either grilled, boiled, or roasted as a cooked vegetable, cold as a salad after cooking and adding oil and vinegar, or raw and shredded, either alone or combined with any salad vegetable. In fact, Jim Harper once said this about beets,

I have some weird habits. For instance, I love beets. Show me a salad bar and I will clean them out of their beets.
- Chris Pratt
